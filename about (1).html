About Us

Welcome to the English-IsiZulu Bilingual Research Dictionary, a comprehensive platform developed to provide accurate and reliable terminology across various languages including isiZulu, isiXhosa, Sepedi, and Sesotho.

Our goal is to bridge the linguistic gap in academic research by offering precise translations of complex research terms, making information accessible to speakers of these languages.

This project was developed by the University of Johannesburg to support scholars and researchers in their pursuit of knowledge by providing a robust multilingual dictionary.

We strive to continually improve the quality and scope of the dictionary, expanding to cover more terms and offering enhanced features, such as pronunciation guides and additional language support.

For more information or assistance, feel free to contact us through the Contact Us page. We welcome your feedback and are committed to making this dictionary a valuable tool for researchers everywhere.
